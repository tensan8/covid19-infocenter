About COVID-19

Sebastian Jern - Pandemic

Coronavirus disease 2019 or also known as COVID-19 is a respiratory syndrome that was caused by a novel coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2. This respiratory illness was first identified in Wuhan, China. A really fast spreading of this virus has made a lot of countries to get into lockdown in order to stop the spreading of this virus. However, COVID-19 becomes worse and on March 11, 2020, World Health Organization (WHO) had declared that this COVID-19 has become a global pandemic, which means that this virus has spread worldwide.

This virus, that was spread by the infected droplets being inhaled by the person who does not have this virus, has caused a lot of loss. There has been more than 125,000,000 confirmed cases of COVID-19 with more than 2,500,000 deaths. There are also around 114 Million people loss their job over 2020, causing some countries to suffer from economic recession. Several ways have been done in order to stop the spread of this virus and to end this pandemic, starting from practicing social distancing, using mask, quarantine, lockdown, and vaccines.

COVID-19 Symptoms

Source: WHO

There are some symptoms of COVID-19 that we need to know. Those who have these symptoms are adviced to look for the medical help. Please avoid any self-diagnosing since it may just spread the wrong information and making more people to feel stressful with this pandemic.

Common Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Dry Cough
  • Fatigue

Less Common Symptoms

  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Nasal congestion
  • Sore throat
  • Headache
  • Muscle or joint pain
  • Chills or dizziness

Severe Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath
  • Lost of appetite
  • Persistent pain in chest
  • High temperature (above 38 ℃)

How to protect yourself from COVID-19

These are several ways that we can do in order to protect us and our family:

  • Wearing a mask when going to public space
  • Praticing physical distancing (Minimum 1 Metre)
  • Avoiding crowd and close contact
  • Keep having a good sanitation
  • Coughing into a bent elbow or tissue

Vaccines

Source: WHO

One of several ways that we can do in order to prevent COVID-19 is by getting vaccinated. A lot of people are still afraid of COVID-19 vaccines because of several reasons. Vaccination is actually a safe and effective way to protect human from harmful diseases. A lot of vaccines have been developed and being used for decades, such as the vaccine for tetanus.

A lot of people are afraid with vaccine because vaccine contains viruses or bacteria. The thing that we need to know here is that, the viruses or the bacteria, that were used for these vaccines, have been weakened. This weakened viruses will train our natural immune system to produce the antibodies, hence we will have a lower probability of being infected.

We should not be worried that the vaccine may make us to have a serious illness. Some side-effects from vaccine such as mild fever or sore arm are normal because our body is still trying to produce the antibodies. All of these vaccines have been tested by professional healt care and being approved by international health organization. Being vaccinated is not only about protecting ourselves, but it will also protect our family and the beloved ones.
